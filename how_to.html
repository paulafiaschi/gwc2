How does it work?

Lenders

Borrowers

01

Pick out your designer clothes

The problem with nice occasion dresses is that we don't want to wear them too often. Give those dresses a new life by creating a listing!

02

Get your listing approved

After you've listed your items, you just need to wait for an approval until your items becomes avaliable for renting.

03

Approve requests

Once your item is listed and approved, it is time to wait for the requests! You have 24 hours to approve or decline the request.

04

Receive payment

When the deal is approved, you will receive your earnings! You will receive 70% of the fee, while the remaining 30% goes to Green Wardrobe Club.

01

Browse and find your favorites

Search by brand, size or price and you'll find a great selection of outfits to choose from! Decide, whether to rent for 3, 5 our 7 days.

02

Pick up your item

Once the item has been approved by the owner, you are ready to go and pick up your outfit in one of the designated pick up points.

03

Enjoy the outfit

Time to shine! Whether it's a birthday party, nice dinner or something completely different, you will be in good hands with the outfit you have borrowed!

04

Return the outfit

Return the item to the pickup point you have previously selected. Don't worry about the dry cleaning - we will do it for you!
